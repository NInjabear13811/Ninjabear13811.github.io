Magneto

Source:Screenrant.com

The rivalry between Wolverine and Magneto is a classic example of the intense and often deadly conflict between two of Marvel's most iconic characters. Their clash has been depicted in various forms, including comics, animated series, and films, each time raising the stakes and showcasing the power struggle between the two. Wolverine's adamantium skeleton, while his greatest strength, also makes him a liability against Magneto's magnetic abilities. Despite this, Wolverine's resilience and willpower often lead to dramatic and unexpected victories over Magneto. The rivalry has been a significant part of the X-Men franchise, influencing the characters and their stories.
