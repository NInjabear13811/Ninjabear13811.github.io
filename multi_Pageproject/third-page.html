The Wolverine:Quick Description The Wolverine is a 2013 superhero film featuring the Marvel Comics character Wolverine. It is the sixth installment in the X-Men film series, the second Wolverine solo film after X-Men Origins: Wolverine (2009), and a spin-off/sequel to X-Men: The Last Stand (2006). Directed by James Mangold, the film stars Hugh Jackman as Logan/Wolverine, alongside Svetlana Khodchenkova, Rila Fukushima, Tao Okamoto, Hiroyuki Sanada, Will Yun Lee, and Famke Janssen. The film follows Logan as he travels to Japan, where he engages in a struggle with an old acquaintance that has lasting consequences. Stripped of his healing powers, Wolverine must battle deadly samurai while grappling with guilt over Jean Grey's death. The film was released by 20th Century Fox in various international markets on July 24, 2013, and in the United States two days later.
